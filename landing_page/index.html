About Us

Bhagalpur College of Engineering is an engineering college located in Bhagalpur, Bihar, India. It was established in 1962 and is affiliated with Aryabhatta Knowledge University. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines like Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics and Computer Science. With experienced faculty and good infrastructure, it is one of the premiere engineering colleges in Bihar.
